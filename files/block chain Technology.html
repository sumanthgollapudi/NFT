Blockchain technology is a decentralized and distributed Ledger that stores the data. This data can be anything like a videos, images. data stored in blocks that are linked together via cryptography Once the block is filled with data it is chained onto the previous block which makes the data change together in chronological order. These blocks are immutable.

Let us see 1 or 2 more advantages why we use blockchain technology

  1. Visibility and traceability:- It can help to track the origins of a variety of items
  2. Security and Privacy:- It creates unalterable record of transactions with end to end encryption
  3. Tokenization:- IN blockchain it was one of the main asset for crypto currency because it process the value of asset and convert into digital tokens which helps the smooth business transactions.
